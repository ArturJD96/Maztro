Maztro

This project presents a novel approach to allow you to intuitively query a database of Mozart's music repertoire using a MIDI keyboard. By allowing you to input a melody, Maztro is able to identify and retrieve pieces in the database that are similar to the query. Resembling a Google for classical music, Maztro allows you to quickly and easily find pieces that resemble what you originally inputted.
